About Me

I'm a certified Massage Therapist with over 5 years of hands-on experience in traditional Thai massage, deep tissue therapy, and holistic wellness techniques. I began my journey at Hot Stone SPA Massage Center , where I developed a strong foundation in therapeutic healing and client care. My approach focuses on relieving physical tension, enhancing relaxation, and restoring the body's natural balance. Over the years, I’ve helped clients manage stress, recover from fatigue, and improve their overall well-being through personalized massage treatments. I completed a full contract at the Hot Stone SPA Massage Center , where I deepened my skills and continued delivering high-quality care. I’m currently working in this field while actively seeking a new opportunity where I can continue supporting others through the power of healing touch.

My Therapeutic Approach

I believe in creating a collaborative, supportive environment where you feel heard and understood. My approach is:

  • Evidence-Based: Using therapies proven effective by research
  • Client-Centered: Tailored to your unique needs and goals
  • Holistic: Considering all aspects of your wellbeing
  • Strengths-Based: Building on your existing resources

During our sessions, you'll gain practical tools to manage challenges while developing deeper self-awareness and resilience.
